    About this course

    The "How to design a mobile app for education" course is designed for people who want to create mobile apps but have no technical experience. This course takes about an hour to complete and requires you to complete all lessons provided. The course consists of 3 lessons and 1 assessment as follows:

    • Lesson 1: Mobile app definition and mobile app development process
    • Lesson 2: Mobile app review
    • Lesson 3: Read specifications, identify items, and draw mockups.
    • Assessment: Mobile app design project - Bike Safety Quiz App

    As you complete the following three steps in turn, you will broaden your understanding of mobile app design and experience the actual steps of designing an app.

    • Step 1: Read the explanation of the concept with the images presented.
    • Step 2: Watch related videos to reinforce what you have learned.
    • Step 3: Take the quiz and follow the provided exercises one by one.


    Mobile App Design Project

    After successful completion of all lessons, a design project should be undertaken. You should read the specified specifications of the mobile app and build a mockup in 20 minutes. You can learn more about this project in our Assessment.
