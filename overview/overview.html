    About this course

    The course, How to design a mobile app for education, is designed for those who want to make a mobile app, but have no experience in technology. It takes around 1 hour to complete this course, and you will be supposed to complete all the provided lessons. This course is composed of the following three lessons and one assessment

    • Lesson 1: Mobile app definition and mobile app development process
    • Lesson 2: Reviewing an mobile app
    • Lesson 3: (Practice) read the specification, identify the items, and draw the mock-up
    • Assessment: Mobile App Design Project - Bike Safety Quiz App

    You will follow the following steps to learn how to design a mobile app.

    • Step 1: Read the written instruction with images about the concepts.
    • Step 2: Watch the relevant videos to reinforce what you have learned.
    • Step 3: Take a quiz to check the degree of your understanding or practice with acrual problems


    Mobile App Design Project

    After successfully completing all the lessons, you are required to carry out a design project. You are required to read the given specification of a mobile app and make a mock-up during 20 minutes. You can learn more about this project at Assessment.
