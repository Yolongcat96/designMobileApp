    About this course

    The course, Designing an Educational Mobile App, is designed for those who want to make a mobile app, but have no experience in technology. It takes around 1 hour to complete this course, and you will be supposed to complete all the provided lessons. This course is composed of the following three lessons.

    • Lesson 1: What is a mobile app?
    • Lesson 2: The overall process of developing a mobile app
    • Lesson 3: Review the existing mobile app

    Each lesson contains three steps.

    • Step 1: read the written instruction with images about the concepts.
    • Step 2: watch the related videos to reinforce what you have learned. Relevant resources are provided for further studies.
    • Step 3: take a quiz to check the degree of how you understand the provided instruction or practice to review the existing mobile apps.


    Design Project

    After successfully completing all the lessons, you will be required to carry out a design project. As an assessment, you will read the provided specification of a mobile app and design the intended mobile app during 20 minutes. You can use paper-pencil, popwer point, photoshop, or illustrator to draw the design. After completing the design, take a picture or scan of the paper version or save the design in the format of pdf and submit it. Feedback will be provided by an instructor.
