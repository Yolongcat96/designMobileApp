    A Mobile App

    What is a mobile app?

    It is a computer program or software application designed to run on a mobile device, such as cell phones, tablets, or apple watches.

    Consider, for example, the Starbucks app. Depending on the location of the customer, it provides various services to customers, such as payment, ordering, menu display, and the nearest store. The Starbucks app is one of the most popular business apps.

    A Mobile App Development

    Overall process of a mobile app development

    The mobile app development process is similar to the normal product development process. First, you come up with an idea for your app, and based on that idea, you determine the overall specifications. Design each page and interface to be drawn on the mobile screen according to your specifications.

    This design is often referred to as a mockup design, and developers write code based on this mockup design. When the code is completed, it is tested internally first, and the process of design-development-test is repeated until the final product is determined. After this process, it is finally released to the App Store or Play Store.
