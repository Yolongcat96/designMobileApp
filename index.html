    Welcome to How to design a mobile app for education

    Do you want to make a mobile app for your teaching or do you want to use a mobile app to make your class more fun and interactive? However, if you don't know how to start it because you have never made an app, this course will help you get started. In this course, you will practice the concept of a mobile app, the entire process of developing a mobile app, and designing a mobile app according to the given specifications step-by-step. As a final assessment, the learner will design a mobile app according to the specifications provided.

    Learning Objectives

    After successfully completing this course, you will be able to:

    • Describe the definition of a mobile app.
    • Identify each step in the mobile app development process.
    • dentify elements on the app screen and draw elements on paper or design tools such as Power Point, Photoshop, Illustrator.
    • Read the mobile app specifications, summarize, and build a list of essentials.
    • Use a pencil or design tools like PowerPoint, Photoshop, or Illustrator to draw the layout of your favorite mobile app.
