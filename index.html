    Welcome to Designing an Educational Mobile App

    Do you want to make a mobile app for your teaching or do you want to make a variety of mobile apps for education? However, if you don't know how to start it because you have never made an app, this course will help you get started. In this course, you will learn the concept of a mobile app, the overall process of developing a mobile app, and finally desining a mobile app based on the specification. This course will give you the opportunity of designing an educational mobile app for writing codes.

    Learning Objectives

    After successfully completing this course, you will be able to:

    • Explain the definition of a mobile app.
    • Identify each step of the process of developing a mobile app.
    • Identify elements of the screen of the actual mobile app and draw them on paper or tools such as Power Point, Photoshop, Illustrator.
    • Read the mobile app specifications and summarize and build a list of the elements you need.
    • Draw the layout of the intended mobile app using a pencil or tools such as power point, photoshop, or illustrator.
